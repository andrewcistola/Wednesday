Description	"This query generates all recorded deaths by the Florida Department of Health where Malignant Neoplasm (C01-C99) was listed as the primary cause of death collected by zip code of residence for the individual regardless of the place fo death. The query includes only deaths for residents of the state of Florida, individuals 25 and older, and the years 2015 to 2019. The deaths are also aggregated by 10 year age range for calculating age adjusted rates. This dataset is publicaly acceesible and available for dissemination."
